About Dublin

Dublin, the capital of Ireland and one of Europe’s most popular city break destinations. Home to over 1 million people Dublin is a bright, colorful, young vibrant city and has a packed calendar of events all year round. The city is the perfect size in that its big enough to spend a few days exploring, but also small enough that you can do most of the exploring on foot. The city has a mild climate which means it is suitable to visit at any time of the year. Whether you’re here for business or pleasure, you are guaranteed to have a fantastic time.
